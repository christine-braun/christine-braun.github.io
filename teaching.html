teaching

ec9c6 - topics in macroeconomics

This is a second year MRes (phd) course at the University of Warwick. The course covers search and matching models of the labor market in dynamic macro model. Topics include search and matching theory, labor market data, and computational methods for solving dynamic labor search models.

ec9c5 - topics in labor economics

This is a second year MRes (phd) course at the University of Warwick. The course covers search and matching model of the labor market and structual estimation of models including maxminimum likelihood methods, generalized method of moments, and simulation methods.
